Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent support issues, we prioritize responses and aim to get back to you as quickly as possible.

Do you offer technical support on weekends?

While our office hours are Monday through Friday, we do monitor urgent support tickets on weekends. For immediate assistance, our help center is available 24/7 with resources and guides.

How can I request a demo for my school or organization?

You can request a demo by selecting "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form subject line. Please include details about your organization and the number of potential users to help us prepare a tailored demonstration.

Where are your offices located?

Our headquarters is in San Francisco, with additional offices in New York, London, and Singapore. All visits are by appointment only, so please contact us in advance if you'd like to schedule a meeting.
